Institute model

A publication system for research that has to survive scrutiny.

Meridian is presented as an editorially serious research institute: calm typography, inspectable methods, sober claims, and a website architecture that can hold reports, dashboards, and governance pages without feeling improvised.

Operating principles

Precision before performance.

The design avoids ornamental futurism. It uses proportion, metadata, negative space, tables, and durable page structures to communicate credibility.

Editorial independenceSeparate research judgement from fundraising, communications, and partner preference.
Review traceMake specialist review and correction status visible whenever relevant.
Evidence postureExpose uncertainty instead of hiding it behind confident prose.
