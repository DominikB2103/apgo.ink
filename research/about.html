About

An editorial research institute for high-consequence systems.

Meridian is a prototype institutional website for publishing rigorous public research with a restrained, serious, and highly legible editorial system.

Institutional posture

Built like a journal. Written like a briefing desk. Structured like an observatory.

The design puts emphasis on typography, hierarchy, source discipline, metadata, and calm visual density. It avoids the startup trope of oversized hero copy, vague gradients, and decorative spectacle.

01

Legibility over theatre

Every visual choice should help a reader understand evidence faster.

02

Judgement with accountability

Opinions are tied to evidence grade, caveats, and explicit decision relevance.

03

Calm under complexity

Dense material is broken into tables, ledgers, cards, and notes without losing authority.
