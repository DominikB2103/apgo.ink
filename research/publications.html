Publication anatomy

Designed for both narrative reading and institutional extraction.

Every serious research page needs more than paragraphs. The components below are intended for consistent metadata, caveats, and decisions.

Evidence

What the claim rests on

Direct measurement, administrative records, expert elicitation, model output, or scenario assumption.

Confidence

How strongly to act

Low, medium, high, or mixed confidence with explicit reason for the rating.

Implication

What it changes

A concise translation into procurement, governance, capital planning, regulation, or research agenda.
