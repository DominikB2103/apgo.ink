Annual agenda

What the desk is trying to resolve.

The programme plan is written as a set of precise editorial questions rather than broad thematic ambition.

Q1 · Evidence baselines

Update desk bibliographies, refresh source quality ratings, and prepare public methods notes.

Q2 · Comparative dossiers

Publish the first full dossiers and release reproducibility appendices for external inspection.

Q3 · Institutional review round

Run specialist critique, policy reader review, and sector-specific decision relevance checks.

Q4 · Observatory synthesis

Convert signal tracking into annual desk summaries and model updates.
