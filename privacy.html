Privacy

A small site with a small data footprint.

APGO currently publishes static pages. It does not operate reader accounts, comments, analytics dashboards or newsletter storage.

SummaryThis siteHostingFontsFunding

This notice describes the website as it exists now. It will be revised before APGO introduces any feature that collects personal information.

What APGO collects

The APGO front end sets no advertising or analytics cookies and contains no account, comment, payment or newsletter database. Copy buttons use your browser’s clipboard interface only when you activate them.

Hosting and delivery

The site is published through GitHub Pages and may be delivered through network infrastructure such as a content-delivery or DNS provider. Those providers may process routine technical logs—including IP address, browser details and request time—under their own policies.

External fonts and links

Pages currently request typefaces from Google Fonts, which causes a connection to Google’s font servers. Links to primary sources, GitHub and Bitcoin wallet software take you to third-party services governed by their own privacy terms.

Bitcoin support

Bitcoin transactions are public on the blockchain. APGO does not ask for donor identity on this site, but a transaction may still be linkable through a wallet, exchange or other service you use.

Changes

Material changes to data collection will be reflected here before or when the relevant feature launches. Last reviewed: 20 July 2026.
