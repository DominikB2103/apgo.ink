Contact

How to reach the newsroom, ask about your membership, or send us something securely.

Meridian is a small newsroom, and every message is read by a person. Use the right channel below and we will get to you faster. If your message concerns a story we have published, a correction or a question about how we reported something, that is the newsroom — not membership — and the addresses differ.

Have a tip?

If you are sharing sensitive information or documents, do not email it. Use our secure tips channel, which explains how to reach us anonymously and protect yourself. Email is not confidential and we cannot promise to shield a source who writes to a general inbox.

Ways to reach us

Each desk has its own address. Writing to the most specific one keeps your message out of a queue and in front of the people who can answer it.

General

General enquiries

Anything that doesn't fit a desk below — partnerships, syndication questions, or a note to the team. hello@meridian.example

Newsroom

Newsroom & tips

Story ideas, feedback on coverage, and questions about our reporting. newsroom@meridian.example. For sensitive material, use the secure tips channel instead.

Corrections

Corrections

Spotted an error? Tell us what's wrong and where. corrections@meridian.example. See how we handle them on our corrections page.

Membership

Membership & billing

Payments, receipts, renewals, cancellations and account access. members@meridian.example. Not a member yet? Join here.

Press

Press & media

Interview requests, comment from the newsroom, and republication permissions. press@meridian.example.

Privacy

Privacy & data

Requests about your personal data and how we use it. privacy@meridian.example. Read our privacy policy.

Response times

We aim to acknowledge most messages within two working days. Membership and billing queries are usually answered the same day, Monday to Friday. The newsroom reads everything but cannot reply to every message individually — if your tip is acted on, a reporter will be in touch. Press requests on deadline should say so in the subject line and we will prioritise them.

If it is sensitive, send it securely. A general inbox is convenient, but it is not confidential — and protecting a source begins with the first message.Meridian newsroom

Before you write

A few answers live elsewhere and may be quicker than an email:

  • Who we are and how we are funded — see About Meridian.
  • How we source, verify and label our reporting — see our editorial standards.
  • How to share documents safely and anonymously — see secure tips.

Whatever the channel, write plainly and tell us what you need. We will do the same.
