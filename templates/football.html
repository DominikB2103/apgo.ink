Method v0.2

From raw event to public score

Designed for scrutiny, not false precision
01

Define coverage

Publish the competitions, providers, missing leagues and minimum 900-minute eligibility rule before naming a player.

02

Compare roles

Evaluate goalkeepers, centre-backs, full-backs, midfield roles, wide attackers and strikers against their own jobs first.

03

Adjust context

Account for possession, game state, opponent strength, minutes and opportunity instead of simply adding per-90 counts.

04

Weight recency

Use a 120-day half-life, capped match-importance multipliers and a transparent team-strength model.

05

Shrink uncertainty

Pull small samples toward the midpoint and show a confidence band, provisional badge and coverage score.

06

Archive every release

Publish weights, model version, changes, corrections and downloadable outputs wherever the data licence permits.

Proposed aggregationIndex = 50 + 10 × confidence × weighted role score

Confidence rises with rolling competitive minutes and stops a brilliant cameo from outranking a year of sustained performance.
