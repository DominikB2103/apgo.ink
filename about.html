About APGO

A small publication with public methods.

APGO is an independent journal and project studio for world affairs, football, books and the systems shaping public life.

On this pageMissionThe workFundingCurrent stage

APGO exists to make complicated things legible without flattening them. The journal is the editorial core; the Football Index and future reference projects turn the same standards—context, transparent judgment and visible revision—into public tools.

Mission

Publish work that respects a reader’s attention. Separate reporting from analysis, label uncertainty, link to original material and explain how rankings or conclusions were produced.

What APGO publishes

Edition 001 begins with three source-backed pieces: a geopolitical analysis of maritime power, an evidence note on football’s crowded calendar and a literary essay connecting Dostoevsky’s Underground Man to online life. The Football Index is a methodology proposal—not yet a live ranking.

Ownership and funding

APGO is currently an independent, early-stage project. Reader support helps pay for research, data, design and infrastructure. A donation does not purchase coverage, removal, access to drafts or a favorable ranking.

Current stage

This is the first public editorial system, not a claim to be a full newsroom. APGO does not yet operate accounts, subscriptions, confidential tip infrastructure or a live statistical ranking. Those capabilities will only be announced when they genuinely exist.
