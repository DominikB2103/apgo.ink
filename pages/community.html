Community standard

Who we want in the room.

We are looking for people who are thoughtful, disciplined, and serious about improving their judgment.

Write clearly

Strong members explain what they think, why they think it, and what would change their mind.

Challenge respectfully

Disagreement is useful when it is specific, calm, evidence-based, and in service of better decisions.

Stay disciplined

We value consistency more than performance theater. Serious thinking beats noise.

Raise the bar

The purpose of the room is not to accumulate people. It is to accumulate standards.
