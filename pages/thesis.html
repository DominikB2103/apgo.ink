Founder thesis

What Tannenhof Mayer is trying to build.

A long-term investment firm built from research discipline, downside awareness, and aligned ownership — starting with a room before a structure.

The problem

Most early communities have enthusiasm but weak standards. Most mature firms have standards but closed doors. Tannenhof Mayer aims to connect the energy of a serious online community with the discipline of an institutional process.

The goal is not to optimize for noise, branding, or hot takes. The goal is to create a room where good work compounds: research, critique, written thinking, and decision-making under uncertainty.

The operating belief

The first asset is not capital. It is judgment. Judgment becomes more reliable when it is written, challenged, and compared against reality over time. That is why the early product is not a fund wrapper — it is a process.

  • Research should be explicit and reviewable.
  • Risk should be designed before returns are celebrated.
  • Incentives should reward contribution and integrity.

The ownership path

The long-term vision is an investment firm owned by many of the people who help build it. That does not mean casual access or symbolic participation. It means a high-standard path where trust is earned through real contribution.

Ownership follows demonstrated quality: written work, judgment, teamwork, reliability, and the ability to improve the room for others.

What the Discord is for

The Discord is the first operating layer: research rooms, sector channels, reviews, reading lists, and introductions. It is where the early standards are set and where the culture of the future firm begins.
